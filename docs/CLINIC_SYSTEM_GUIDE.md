## How It Works: The Simple Flow

### **Step 1: Patient Registration**
1. Patient visits your website
2. Fills out basic information (name, email, phone)
3. Completes detailed medical registration form
4. System creates secure patient profile

### **Step 2: Appointment Request**
1. Patient selects preferred doctor
2. Chooses date and time
3. Adds appointment reason and any notes
4. Submits request (takes 30 seconds)

### **Step 3: Your Admin Dashboard**
1. You see new appointment requests immediately
2. Review patient details and appointment reason
3. Click "Schedule" to confirm
4. System automatically sends SMS confirmation to patient

### **Step 4: Patient Receives Confirmation**
1. Patient gets SMS: "Your appointment is confirmed for [date/time] with Dr. [Name]"
2. Patient can view appointment details anytime
3. Easy to reschedule or cancel if needed
